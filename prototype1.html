Prototype 1

Overview

This was the first attempt at making a protective case for the ESP32‑C3 Microprocessor. I went with a rectangular design 2mm walls all around. A snap on lid, i also put in 2 support columns in the case that will hold up the microprocessor so that only 1 mm of the pins will stick out to help avoid damage to the pins. I also put in a hole in the lid to get access to the USB-C port.

Key Features

  • Basic rectangular shell
  • Simple USB‑C cutout
  • 2 support columns
  • Snap‑on lid concept

Issues Found

  • USB‑C port misaligned by 1mm and the oval shape didnt help with fitting in the USB-C.
  • No ventilation holes
  • Misalligned holes for reset/boot buttons by 1mm.
  • Couldn't see if the ESP32‑C3 could even fit in the Case due to no where for the pins to go out of.
  • No pin access.
