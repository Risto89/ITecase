Final Product

Overview

Most of the improvements mentioned on Prototype 2 have been adjusted and improved the design by alot, there is still acouple of improvements i would do to improve it. As it stands now the lid fits and is the same length on all sides and fits the case which was shortened by 1mm but the connectivity could be better. USB-C slot is better positioned and lines up perfectly , buttons base is perfect now and can close properly with the buttons functioning as they should while also acting as 2 mini support bases. The pins access points were widened by 1mm and shortened by 1mm to further more secure the ESP32‑C3 and allow the jumpers to fit in there when connected and now they can comfortably. The final version of the ESP32‑C3 protective case is a polished, functional enclosure ready for everyday use. It combines durability and accessibility.

Final Features

  • Perfect USB‑C alignment
  • Strong 2 mm walls
  • Improved ventilation pattern
  • Secure snap‑fit lid
  • Wide GPIO access slots for jumper access
  • 2 funtional button extensions
