Prototype 2

Overview

Prototype 2 improved on the first design by fixing an existing issue with the USB-C slot where it didn't print out the shape properly and found a more suitable shape thats in line with the USB-C design. Also adding in missing key components such as 11 3x3 mm triangular ventilation holes ontop of the case and 7 along the back. pin access slots along the bottom of the case that were 24mm long and 2mm wide that i didn't add in the first prototype. Developed 2 buttons with a square base 6x6 mm and 2mm thick and long cylinder tubes 4.5x4.5 mm and 4 mm high to fit inside the button holes to make up button extensions in a way. Some how i made the case 1mm longer than the lid probably when i was putting in ventilation slots in Tinkercad so the lid doesn't fit the case at all, also the lid is 0.10mm longer on 3 of the sides so the lid isn't connecting properly.

Improvements

  • Corrected USB‑C alignment by 1mm and finding a better and more suitable shape that fits the USB-C perfectly.
  • Added pin access to bottom of the case
  • Added ventilation slots on top of the case and along back wall of the case.
  • Button access holes were made 1mm larger all round
  • Developed 2 buttons to press the existing buttons on the microcontroller alot more easier.
  • Fixed the length on the lid so all sides are the same.

Remaining Problems

  • Lid connection is weaker than prototype 1
  • Case is 1mm to long so needs to be shortened.
  • Pins access slots needed widening from 2mm to 3mm because the jumpers can't fit in.
  • Base of the buttons were too big to close the case properly so reduced the square base from 2mm thick to 1mm.
