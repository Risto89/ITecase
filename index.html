ESP32‑C3 Protective Case Project

Welcome

This website documents the full development journey of a custom protective case designed for the ESP32‑C3 microcontroller board. From early sketches to the final 3D‑printed enclosure, each stage shows how the design evolved.

Project Goals

  • Protect the ESP32‑C3 from dust, impacts, and short circuits.
  • Allow full access to USB‑C, buttons, and GPIO pins.
  • Create a compact, easy‑to‑print enclosure.
  • Iterate through prototypes to improve fit and usability.
